Find your fit

Size guide.

Take a few minutes to measure yourself for the most accurate fit. All measurements in inches.

How to measure
1
Chest — Measure under your armpits, around the fullest part of your chest. Keep the tape level.
2
Waist — Measure your natural waist (about an inch above your belly button). Keep one finger between the tape and your skin for breathing room.
3
Hips — Measure around the fullest part of your hips, usually 7-9 inches below your waist.
4
Inseam — Measure from the inside of your leg at the crotch down to where you want the pants to end (typically the top of your shoe).
5
Foot length — Stand against a wall on a piece of paper. Mark the back of your heel and your longest toe, measure the distance.
