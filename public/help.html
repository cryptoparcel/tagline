Tracking

You'll get a tracking number by email as soon as your order ships. You can also see live status from your account page.

Returns & Exchanges

30-day exchange window

If your fit isn't right, we'll exchange it within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, with tags attached.

Final sale items

Custom orders, personalized pieces, and items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged.

How to start a return

Email us at our contact page with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a prepaid label and instructions.

Lifetime repair

Our pieces are built to last. If something tears, comes apart at the seams, or has a manufacturing defect — at any point in the future — send it back and we'll repair it free of charge.
